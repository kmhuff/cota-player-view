Campaign Introduction

Â 9th April 2023 at 11:22am
Player Utility

For generations, the inhabitants of the town of Voidperch have lived a small life. Less than 2,000 people trapped on a small floating rock in an infinite night. You struggle to pull enough food from the limited farming space to feed everyone, and must occasionally fend off monsters that wing in from the black nothingness.

It is a hard, boring struggle for survival, but it's all any of you have ever known.

It's more difficult for the elders, you sometimes feel. They still tell stories of Skybridge, the land of your many-times-great-grandparents. Golden highways crossing the sky, legions of knights in burnished armor, enough food that anyone who wasn't careful would end up fat.

It always sounded like a fairy tale to child of the strict limits of Voidperch. In the aftward direction, there is nothing but a drop off the floating island into an infinite abyss. In the foreward, Hadriel's Wall blocks all progress.

Or at least... it did.

For centuries, the Angel Hadriel always insisted that he was protecting the people of Voidperch by maintaining the Wall to the fore. Over the last few years, however, it became clear that he was ailing, and the Wall with him. Its bright gold glow faded slowly down to a faint yellow sputter.

Two weeks ago, Hadriel descended into Voidperch's town square, calling all who could to listen to him.

"I am dying," he told you all, his commanding voice from your childhood now hoarsened into a rasp. "And with me, your protection will also die. In two weeks, you will be required to be in control of your own destiny again. I am sorry."

The announcement provoked every reaction from panic to elation, but for whatever reason, you found yourself curious. There was land beyond the Wall. Beyond Voidperch. Perhaps a daring explorer might even discover the Skybridge of legends, and make a legend of themselves in the process.

Today, wearing the best equipment Voidperch can scrounge up, surrounded by your companions, you step forward into the unknown as the golden Angelic light of the Wall fades for the last time.
